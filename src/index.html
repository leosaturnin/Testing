About

OUR MISSION

We are called to fulfill the Great Commission throughout the world until Jesus returns. We believe that everyone should have an opportunity to hear the Gospel of Christ.
“Therefore, go and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit. Teach these new disciples to obey all the commands I have given you.” Matthew 28:19-20

MISSION STATEMENT

As an instrument of God, Global Assist is dedicated to proclaiming the Good News of Jesus Christ worldwide. Through the work of our International Ministry Partners we are committed to fulfilling His Great Commission by the making of disciples, who through the power of the Holy Spirit, build faith communities that transform the human condition.

Programs

We know there are billions of people that have yet to accept Christ as their Lord and Savior. Because of the great need and our belief to follow the great commission, we are ready to go wherever God directs us. We understand that each culture is unique and therefore we will always work through established and emerging indigenous global ministries to deliver the Word of God. God is supernaturally calling these leaders from all over the world to raise up a ministry that reaches their regions. It is this strategy that we believe God is using to reach massive numbers of humanity for His Kingdom. It's a dynamic strategy that can grow quickly enough to keep up with the surge in the human population.

The Church Assist program is dedicated to providing the network, logistics and resources to American churches helping them to fulfill their Godly global missions responsibility.


Community Assist

The Community Assist program will be dedicated to providing humanitarian resources to meet the physical needs of people and communities.

CHILDREN ASSIST

Children Assist is dedicated to providing help and resources to drive education for children in locations of need.
